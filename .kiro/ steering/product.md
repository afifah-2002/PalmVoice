# PalmVoice Product Overview

## Purpose
Retro Palm Pilot productivity app combining task management with Tamagotchi-style pet care. Users complete tasks to earn coins and keep their virtual pet alive.

## Target Users
Students and young professionals who need productivity motivation with a nostalgic gaming twist.

## Key Features
- Palm Pilot aesthetic with authentic pixelated design
- Virtual pet system (Cat, Puppy, Panda, Koala)
- Task management with app integrations
- Coin economy rewarding productivity
- Theme customization and pet shop

## Business Goals
- Gamify productivity to increase task completion
- Create emotional attachment through pet care
- Build habit formation through daily streaks
